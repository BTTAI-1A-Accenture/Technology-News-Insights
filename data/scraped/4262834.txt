Driver: Increasing Initiatives to Promote Green Building Construction is a Key Revenue Growth Driving Factor
Increasing initiatives to promote construction of green buildings in order to prevent environmental degradation is expected to boost market growth during the forecast period. Goal of green buildings is to increase the efficiency of usage of resources such as water, energy, and materials. These also aim to reduce negative impacts on the environment during the building's entire life cycle. These energy-efficient buildings require low maintenance, last longer, and guarantee the maintenance of overall structural & thermal performance over time. These also require less energy and create low CO2 emissions. The durability, lightweight nature, recyclability, and strength of plastic materials make these ideal for the construction of green buildings.
Restraint: Concerns Associated with Use of Plastics is a Key Growth Restraining Factor
Construction industry is considered to be the second-largest user of plastics, with 20% of plastic waste coming from this sector. Serious concerns associated with the use of plastics is expected to restrain market growth during the forecast period. Global pollution from plastics is estimated to increase by approximately 3% each year. Demands pertaining to circular economies and mitigation of climate change is restricting the use of certain types of plastics. Presence of micro- and nano-sized plastics in marine life across oceans is contributing to rising marine pollution.  Who is winning?
