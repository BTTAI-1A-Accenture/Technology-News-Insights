Thermal Oil Technology: A Brief Overview Thermal oil technology involves the use of heat transfer fluids (often referred to as thermal oils) to transfer and store heat energy. This method provides a number of advantages over traditional steam or water-based systems. It's more efficient, flexible, and can operate at lower pressures, making it safer and easier to maintain. Plus, thermal oil systems are known for their ability to deliver uniform, precise temperature control. The Heart of the Matter: INNERGY's Coil Design Technology At the heart of any thermal oil boiler is the coil design, which plays a pivotal role in heat exchange efficiency and overall system performance.
