Feb 7, 2023 Jane Miller, of Jersey Shore, passed away Friday evening Feb. 3, 2023 at the Gatehouse Hospice in Williamsport.   She was born in Pine Creek, the youngest and last living child of ten children born to Harry and Esther Armstrong Bowers. Her husband Terrance L. “Ted” Miller predeceased her. Jane’s survivors include her daughter, Janelle Miller, of Avis; a grandson, Wayne Houseknecht; and two great granddaughters, Hannah and Camrynne. In Keeping with Jane’s wishes her Life Tribute Services will be held on Wednesday, Feb. 8, 2023 at noon at the Jersey Shore Cemetery Chapel.
