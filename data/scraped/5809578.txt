Holding: An insurer with financial responsibility for bankruptcy claims is a “party in interest” under 11 U.S.C. § 1109(b) that “may raise and may appear and be heard on any issue” in a Chapter 11 case. Judgment: Reversed and remanded, 8-0, in an opinion by Justice Sotomayor on June 6, 2024. Justice Alito took no part in the consideration or decision of the case. ABOUT
