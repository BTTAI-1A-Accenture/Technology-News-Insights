Improving access to programs and resources to help young people recover, build resilience, strengthen community networks and improve mental health and wellbeing. The Clarence Valley Close the Gap - Resilience the Courage to Come Back project was held by the Mudyala Aboriginal Corporation with funding from the Children and Young People Wellbeing Recovery Initiative. We pay respect to the Traditional Custodians and First Peoples of NSW, and acknowledge their continued connection to their country and culture.
