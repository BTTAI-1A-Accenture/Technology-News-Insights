Karur Vysya Bank executed an agreement as an additional bancassurance partner in the life insurance category with SBI Life on Thursday. Bancassurance is an arrangement between a bank and an insurance firm through which the insurer sell its products to the bank's customers. Recently, Bharti AXA Life Insurance, a joint venture between Bharti Enterprises and global insurance firm AXA, entered into a bancassurance tie-up with Unity Small Finance Bank (Unity Bank) in order to sell its life insurance products through the lender's 111 branches of the network.
