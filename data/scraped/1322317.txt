Trooper shot in arm A 23-year-old man accused of shooting a Mississippi Highway Patrol trooper is on the run.  The trooper was shot in the arm at about 7 a.m. Tuesday on U.S. 61 in Bolivar County. Officials with the Mississippi Department of Public Safety said the trooper's injuries were not life-threatening. The suspect, Stanley Self Jr., is believed to be in the area of Isaac Street and South Street in Mound Bayou. Self is believed to be armed and dangerous, MHP officials said. Self was last seen wearing a white T-shirt and khaki pants. He weighs 165 pounds, is 5 feet and 9 inches tall and has brown eyes and black hair. Anyone who sees Self is asked to call 911. Anyone who has information about Self's whereabouts is asked to call 1-855-485-TIPS or send an email to MBITIPS@dps.ms.gov.  The Mississippi Bureau of Investigation is at the scene gathering evidence.
