 Tifin Wealth is extending access to its personalized investment platform at no cost to Financial Planning Association members with under $100 million in assets under management, Tifin and FPA said Wednesday. The purpose of this new member benefit is to “empower financial planners in the FPA community with cutting-edge tools and resources to better serve their clients while making innovative investment technology more accessible,” Tifin and FPA said in a joint announcement.
