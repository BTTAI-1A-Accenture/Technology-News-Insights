PITTSBURGH, May 25, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- "I wanted to create a new game to enjoy outdoors with family and friends," said an inventor, from Amherstburg, Ontario, Canada, "so I invented the FRIZBAR. My design would offer versatility for all ages and skill levels and it can be set up at any outdoor location." 



 The patent-pending invention provides an outdoor game for fresh air entertainment. In doing so, it increases fun and entertainment. It also could spark friendly competition and social interaction. The invention features a portable design that is easy to play so it is ideal for the general population. Additionally, it can be set up in any outdoor home or remote location such as a yard, park, beach or campsite and a prototype model is available upon request. The original design was submitted to the Toronto sales office of InventHelp. It is currently available for licensing or sale to manufacturers or marketers.
