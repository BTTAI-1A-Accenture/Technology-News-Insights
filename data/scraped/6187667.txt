Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure Veteran to Scale Offering as Enterprises Increasingly Turn to Graph as a Cloud Database System of Record SAN MATEO, Calif., April 4, 2023 /CNW/ -- Neo4j®, the graph database and analytics leader, announced today that Sudhir Hasbe has joined the company's executive leadership team as Chief Product Officer (CPO). Hasbe will oversee the company's software portfolio across its native graph database and data science offerings, reporting directly to CEO and Co-Founder Emil Eifrem.
