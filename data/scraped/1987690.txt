Logistics hub Zhengzhou, Central China's Henan Province, sent its first international cargo train to Laos via the China-Laos Railway on March 15, 2022. The China-Laos Railway celebrated its 100th day of operation last week, having carried more than 1.2 million tons of goods and injected fresh momentum into economic and trade cooperation between China and ASEAN.
