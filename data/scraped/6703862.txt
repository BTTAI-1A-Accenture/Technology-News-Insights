 The Tri-County Technology Consortium recently announced that applications for the 2023 TC2 Scholarship are now open for graduating high school seniors. Three $1,000 scholarship awards will be available to graduating seniors in Armstrong, Butler and Indiana counties who will be pursuing a degree, diploma or certificate in a post-secondary information technology or technology education program. Applications can be found at tricountywib.org. 514 West Wayne Street Butler, PA 16001
