The XRP Ledger (XRPL) has shown significant growth in multiple areas of its protocol during the second quarter of 2023, according to a recent report by crypto analytics platform Messari.
The report indicates that XRP's market capitalization has increased by 42.5% year-to-date. The increase was prompted by the asset's price increase in the first quarter; however, in the second quarter, market capitalization decreased by 10.7%, from $27.8 billion to $24.8 billion.
Although the XRP platform experienced a decline in transaction volume quarter-over-quarter, average daily nonfungible token (NFT) transactions increased by 12.7%, from 13,800 to 15,600.
While the XRPL has a strong presence in the decentralized finance (DeFi) and non-fungible token (NFT) ecosystems, it is often overshadowed by top competitors such as Ethereum and Solana.
Nonetheless, there are indications that this trajectory is beginning to change. Messari data revealed an additional significant development in the XRP ecosystem, namely the expansion of XRPL sidechains with the recent introduction of two notable protocols, Coreum and Root Network.
