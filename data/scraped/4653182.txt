DAVENPORT, Iowa (KWQC) - CASI is hosting its 41st annual St. Patrick’s Day Race on March 18 at 9 a.m. According to CASI, the race is the group’s largest fundraiser supporting our mission all year long. Registration is open now. Packet pick-up will be on March 17 from Noon - 6:00 p.m. at CASI, 1035 West Kimberly Road or on race day starting at 7 a.m. at the  DoubleTree by Hilton -  Davenport, 111 E. 2nd Street.  After the race, Cody Road will be at the Afterparty inside the DoubleTree. Copyright 2023 KWQC. All rights reserved.
