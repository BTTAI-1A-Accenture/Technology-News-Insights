  Growing Concern for the Rising Road Crashes to Drive Growth of Global Automotive Adaptive Cruise Control Market  
The global automotive adaptive cruise control market is estimated to grow majorly on account of the increased concern about the rising incidences of road crashes which according to the World Health Organization is estimated to be over 1 million deaths every year. Further, every 1% increase in mean speed produces a 3% and 4% increase in the serious crash risk and fatal crash risk respectively.
