Publisher CFK and developer TALESshop will release management and dating simulation game Miracle Snack Shop for PlayStation 4 on January 26, the companies announced. It will support English, Korean, and Japanese language options.  Miracle Snack Shop first launched for PC via Steam on December 26, 2018, followed by Switch on December 2, 2021. Here is an overview of the game, via CFK: After facing some major adversity, Seolhwa Cha ends up owning a small snack shop.  There is just one problem: business is dead. Seolhwa doesn’t have time to sing the blues though because in his attic something has appeared… A portal? Out from that portal walks… a queen from another world?! As Seolhwa serves the first bowl of shaved ice to Philia, also known as Queen Salt… He takes his first step toward growing closer to her… What are you waiting for, Ice Sculptor? The queen wishes for shaved ice. A frozen treat of a story that will make your heart pound, and it all begins in your snack shop! Watch the PlayStation 4 announcement trailer below.
