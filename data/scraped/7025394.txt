					Philippines' Technology News and Reviews				 Just days after its first introduction, the new Infinix Hot 30i has already made its way to the Philippines through the brand’s official stores in Shopee and Lazada. The new Infinix Hot 30i is powered by a UNISOC T606 processor with 4GB of RAM and 128GB of expandable storage. A budget device, it only has an HD+ for the 6.6-inch IPS display, but it does bosts a 90Hz refresh rate. There’s a standard 5,000mAh battery with a decent 18W adapter that plugs via the USB Type-C port. What’s more, Infinix put a 5-megapixel front camera, and a 13-megapixel on the back with an AI lens, and ships with Android 12-based XOS 12. It also managed to have a side-mounted fingerprint scanner and 4G LTE. Due to the price limitations, it didn’t get to have 5G connectivity. The Infinix Hot 30i has an SRP of Php5,599 in the Philippines but is currently listed for only Php4,299 on both Shopee and Lazada. You can get it in three colors: Black, White, and Blue.
