   BASF has signed a long-term agreement to obtain biobased 1,4-butanediol from Qore, a joint venture of Cargill and Helm. Qore is building a $300 million plant in Eddyville, Iowa, that will make butanediol from corn rather than fossil fuels. BASF plans to use the chemical to make derivatives such as the spandex intermediate polytetrahydrofuran.
