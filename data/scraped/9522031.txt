The movie will cross the milestone in just over six days, a first for Indian cinema The Shah Rukh Khan, John Abraham and Deepika Padukone-starrer ‘Pathaan’ is on a record-breaking run at the domestic and overseas box office as it has registered Rs 5910 million in gross collections around the world in six days. The film, directed by Siddharth Anand, released on January 25. ‘Pathaan’, on its sixth day, collected Rs 265 million in India (Hindi - Rs 255 million, all dubbed versions - Rs 10 million). The overseas gross on day six is Rs 160 million. In six days, ‘Pathaan’ has recorded Rs 2246 million in overseas territories alone, while nett collection in India stand at Rs 3,072.5 million (Hindi - Rs 2965 million, dubbed - Rs 107.5 million).
