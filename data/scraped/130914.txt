Stock photoKlaus Vedfelt | Getty Images Syracuse, N.Y. — Eight Central New York high schools had 90% or more of their students test as proficient on the Algebra I Regents exam in 2022 and 18 had at least 90% scored proficient on the Algebra II test, according to state data. The No. 1 ranked school in the region on the Algebra I test was Cincinnatus High School in Cortland County with 100% of its students scoring proficient.
