
  The education and research at the School of Global Studies provide the ability to understand and analyse social phenomena in a global world. Our education is characterized by its high societal relevance, and our researchers strive to make our global world understandable through a unique interdisciplinary research environment. We do research within the fields of Peace and Development Studies, Environmental Social Science, Human Rights and Social Anthropology.
