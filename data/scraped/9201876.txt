globest.com/2023/01/31/stonehenge-buys-upper-east-side-tower-for-115m/   Swiss bank UBS sells 32-atory, 196-unit RiverEast for $587K/unit. Stonehenge NYC has acquired a 32-story, 196-unit multifamily at 408 E. 92nd Street for $115M, a deal that translates to about $587K/unit. According to reports, the deal for the tower—known as RiverEast—is still covered by the 421a tax credits that expired last year for new developments. The new owner is required to keep 25% of the units rent stabilized until 2026. At that point, the owner will have to option to extend the commitment to maintain affordable units for another 15 years or convert all of the apartments to market-rate housing.
