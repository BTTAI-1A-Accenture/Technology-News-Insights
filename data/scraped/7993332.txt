CENTER TWP. - Central Valley School District will host its second annual Chorus Fest on Tuesday. The concert begins at 6 p.m. in front of the high school. Concertgoers may bring a chair and enjoy refreshments from the Papa Dukes and Bruster's food trucks. Songs will range from musical theater and choral standards to oldies and today's hits.
