Before we delve into the top companies to work for in sales, it’s worth exploring why remote selling has become such a significant trend. With the pandemic forcing many businesses to shift their operations online, remote selling has become an essential tool for sales professionals across industries. Remote selling refers to the process of selling products or services without physically meeting the customer. This can be done through various channels, including email, phone calls, video conferencing, and social media. Remote selling has several advantages over traditional face-to-face sales. For one, it allows sales professionals to reach a wider audience without being limited by geographical location. It also allows for more efficient use of time and resources, as sales professionals can connect with multiple clients in a shorter amount of time.
