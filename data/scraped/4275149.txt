Dive into the lessons she has learned while navigating her own transitions, and gain insights on how to build resilience, creativity, and adaptability in the face of constant change. Don't let the shifting sands of the future of work hold you back—embrace it and thrive on the challenges it presents. Dr. Gabriella Rosen Kellerman is an accomplished expert in the field of resilience and emotional intelligence. With a background in psychiatry, she transitioned to the tech industry to use software as a tool for improving well-being and reducing stress in the workplace. As a key player at BetterUp, Gabriella has held numerous leadership roles, including Chief Product Officer, Chief Innovation Officer, and Head of BetterUp Labs. Additionally, she co-founded LifeLink and has provided valuable insights to companies in healthcare, coaching, and behavioral change technology. Gabriella is the co-author, with Martin Seligman, of the new book Tomorrowmind: Thriving at Work with Resilience, Creativity, and Connection―Now and in an Uncertain Future.  In order to thrive in the evolving world of work, it's essential for professionals to anticipate future changes, adapt accordingly, and have a plan in place. This foresight enables individuals to stay ahead of the curve, acquire new skills, and remain relevant in their industry. Embracing a growth mindset and seeking out new opportunities can help professionals prosper amidst uncertainty. During our conversation, Dr. Gabriella Rosen Kellerman emphasized the importance of such adaptability and openness to change. Drawing from her experiences at BetterUp, she shared insights on how businesses and individuals can successfully plan for the future by fostering soft skills, developing a sense of purpose, and seizing new opportunities as they arise. The ability to adapt to ongoing changes in the world of work is crucial for professionals who want to stay relevant and successful. This is why the insights shared in this episode are so crucial for people like you. Emphasizing the development of resilience, creativity, and adaptability will empower you to face the challenges of a shifting workplace with confidence. Just to let you know, you're not alone on this journey; together, we can all foster a brighter future in our professional lives.
