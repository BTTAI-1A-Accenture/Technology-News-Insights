Para-Mallam, who is the team leader of a delegation of the National Institute for Policy and Strategic Studies, Group 1, Senior Executive Course 45, 2023, Kuru, Jos, Para-Mallam, said the eventual completion of the airport would help the state achieve its potential as an agric entrepreneurial state.
She stated that the agro cargo airport would further open up business opportunities for both local and foreign investors, thereby solidifying its status as the industrial capital of the country.
This was disclosed in a statement that documented the visit of a delegation from the NIPSS to the airport site, which was led by the Special Adviser for Works and Infrastructure, Ade Akinsanya.
