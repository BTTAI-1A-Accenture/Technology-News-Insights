  One man is dead and another injured after a reported shooting on Highway 58 in Chattanooga early Friday morning, police said. No arrests have been made in connection with the shooting, according to police. Officers responded to reports of a shooting around 1:30 a.m. at a building that includes the Studio 58 Sports Bar, according to a news release. They found one man dead at the scene, the release said. A second man who had been shot was taken to a hospital in a personal car, police said. His condition is unknown.
