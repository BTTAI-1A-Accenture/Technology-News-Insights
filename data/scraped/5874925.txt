Newark, New Jersey - July 10, 2023 - Recent statistics indicate that African American Land Ownership in America stands at .33%, not even 1%! These figures highlight the extreme urgency in advancing economic opportunities for black individuals and the apparent challenges in achieving equitable representation. The Own Real Estate Seminar seeks to explore these statistics, identify barriers to ownership, and equip attendees with the knowledge and tools necessary to navigate the real estate market effectively. Event Details:  Free / Own Real Estate Seminar Date: Saturday, July 22, 2023 Time: 10 A.M. - 2 P.M. Venue: Shaquille O'neal City Plex Movie Theatre, 360-394 Springfield Ave,  Newark, New Jersey Key Highlights of the seminar include: 1. Ownership Empowerment Discussions: A Surprise Guest Speaker Who has worked with Coca Cola, Nabisco, AT&T, Universal Music, Pierre Cardin, Macy's along with Major Real Estate Projects In (NYC & NJ) Etc  will lead engaging discussions on the significance of economic development within communities. These discussions will highlight the importance of homeownership, generational wealth, and the long-term benefits of real estate investments in building a strong financial foundation. 2. Strategies for Wealth Accumulation: will be conducted to provide attendees with practical strategies for building generational wealth, including retirement planning, vacation property investment strategies, and other avenues. This will focus on leveraging real estate as a vehicle for long-term financial stability and ensuring a prosperous future for families and communities. 3. Expert Insights and Advice: Participants will have the opportunity to gain valuable insights and advice on homeownership and economic development. 4. Networking and Collaboration: The event will facilitate networking and collaboration among attendees, allowing for the exchange of ideas, experiences, and resources. This collaborative environment will foster meaningful connections and empower individuals to tap into the collective strength of their community in pursuit of economic advancement. "We are thrilled to host this free Real Estate Seminar event, which aims to address ownership, promote economic development within the community, and emphasize the importance of generational wealth," said Mr. Lake, Communication Director at Own Real Estate Development. "By providing education, strategies, and networking opportunities, we hope to empower attendees to seize the potential of real estate to secure a prosperous future and unlock new avenues of financial growth." The Own Real Estate Seminar event is open to all individuals from all backgrounds who are interested in learning more about economic development and the wealth-building potential of real estate.
