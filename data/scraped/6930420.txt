This becomes crucial while sending files to cloud storage, emailing large attachments, or sharing media on social platforms. In contrast, download speed defines the speed of data delivery from the internet to your device - essential for streaming videos, downloading files, or simply browsing the web. In most cases, the upload speed is a fraction of the download speed. However, this traditional disparity is where Kloud9 makes a difference.
