A project of  A project of  Journalists with an interest in revenue and audience development can register for this online training. The Google News Initiative (GNI) is seeking applications for the Digital Transformation Labs, a series of training sessions that seeks to promote business strategies for the news industry. The program will offer over 200 hours of training and consulting sessions across three labs: audience development, reader revenue and ads revenue.  Each quarter a new cohort starts the lab. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis, but places are limited.
