 Arthur J. Gallagher & Co. has announced the acquisition of Nebraska-based Quality First Insurance. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed. Quality First serves healthcare and senior living facilities in Nebraska and around the state. As part of the transaction, Quality First president Kathy Mock and her team will relocate to Gallagher’s Omaha office and report to Ryan Isaacs.
