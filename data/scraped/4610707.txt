Welcome to the Operations & Information Systems Department! We offer programs leading to both undergraduate and graduate degrees. At the undergraduate level, our students can earn a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Management Information Systems or Analytics & Operations Management. At the graduate level, we offer a Master of Science degree in Business Analytics, a Ph.D. in Business Administration with a concentration in Management Information Systems and Management Science and graduate certificates in Business Analytics and Supply-Chain & Operations Management.
