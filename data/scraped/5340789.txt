HOTEL 101 Group held its simultaneous Christmas tree-lighting at Hotel 101 Manila, together with its properties at Jinjiang Inn Ortigas, Jinjiang Inn Makati, Jinjiang Inn Boracay Station 1, and Injap Tower Hotel in Iloilo last Nov. 11, 2022. "We are happy to see our partners who were with us also at our very first Christmas tree-lighting same time last year, as we have continued and strengthened these partnerships, as well as made new ones as we bounced back better from the pandemic," Hotel 101 Group General Manager Gel Gomez said as welcome guests at the Manila property.
