newsCrime By Zaeem Shaikh and Jamie LandersStaff Writers By Zaeem Shaikh and Jamie Landers Staff Writers 10:06 PM on Jun 29, 2023 CDT — Updated at 6:32 AM on Jun 30, 2023 CDT Original post: Authorities on Thursday evening issued an Amber Alert for two girls believed to be missing in Henderson County. The Henderson County Sheriff’s Office issued an alert for Bonnie Webb, 11, and Willow Webb, 14. According to the alert, the girls were last seen about 4:15 p.m. in the 4300 block of County Road 1408 in Athens, located about 70 miles southeast of Dallas. Bonnie Webb is 4-11 and about 75 pounds with brown hair and blue eyes. She was last seen wearing a dark-colored shirt and blue jeans.
