 Global Multi-touch Screen Market Size The global multi-touch screen market size reached US$ 11.8 Billion in 2022. Looking forward, IMARC Group expects the market to reach US$ 25.4 Billion by 2028, exhibiting a CAGR of 13.07% during 2023-2028. A multi-touch screen is a display device that senses and responds to multiple gestures and pressures at different locations on a single screen. It utilizes several technologies such as acoustic, resistive, capacitive, infrared, and optical and is commonly used in smartphones, laptops, personal computers (PCs), kiosks, and tablets. It consists of a controller, a capacitive touch sensor, a digital signal processor (DSP), and an application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC). It is also faster than conventional touch screens, can distinguish between various touch inputs, convenience and speed, is easier to use, and provides enhanced functionality. As a result, multi-touch screens are extensively employed across the education, retail, government, media, and consumer electronics industries.
