The Asia Cup 2023 marks the 16th instalment of the prestigious One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament, meticulously organized by the Asian Cricket Council (ACC). Commencing on August 30, 2023, this cricketing extravaganza is set to run until September 17, 2023. In the 2023 Asia Cup, six formidable teams - India, Bangladesh, Afghanistan, Pakistan, Nepal, and the reigning champions, Sri Lanka - will battle it out for a coveted spot in the grand final. The competition features a concise schedule of 13 matches. The format of the Asia Cup 2023 is refreshingly straightforward. Out of the six participating teams, four will progress to the Super 4s stage, where they will engage in fierce contests.
