Granzella Game Factory, formerly Granzella Visual, is a video game developer headquartered in Fukue, Showa Ward, Nagoya, Aichi, Japan, and a subsidiary of Granzella. It is known for Disaster Report 4 Plus: Summer Memories, City Shrouded in Shadow, and more.
