PITTSBURGH, March 1, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- "I wanted to create a unique cover option for a swamp cooler," said an inventor, from Delta, Colo., "so I invented the COOL COVERS. My design could express support for various organizations, celebrate holidays or simply enhance the appearance of the unit." 



 The invention provides a personalized cover for a swamp cooler air conditioning unit. In doing so, it enables the user to decorate or customize the look of the unit. It also could provide added protection during the winter months and it can be used to display support for various charities and organizations. The invention features a novel design that is easy to apply and use so it is ideal for households. Additionally, it is producible in design variations. The original design was submitted to the National sales office of InventHelp. It is currently available for licensing or sale to manufacturers or marketers.
