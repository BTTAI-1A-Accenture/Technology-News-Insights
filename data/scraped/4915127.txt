         Scammers claiming to be NFU Energy suppliers are targeting farm businesses, the union has warned. NFU Energy provides advice and practical support to rural businesses and acts as a buying group to achieve discounts for its members. The scam companies attempt to pressure farmers into signing new contracts. However, the union warns that these companies have no relation to the NFU or NFU Energy and should be ignored.
