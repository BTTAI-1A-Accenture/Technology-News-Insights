WHEELING, W.Va., Aug. 3, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- The Wheeling, WV based Main Street Financial Services Corp. reported its second quarter year to date financial results on Thursday, August 3, 2023. 



 Year to date Revenue was $13.48 million compared to $10.44 million in 2022, Year to date Net Income as $3.60 million compared to $2.42 million in 2022. Total assets came in at $597.3 million in 2023 versus $589.9 million in 2022. Gross loans totaled $418.4 million in 2023 versus $401.7 million in 2022. Total deposits totaled $493.3 million in 2023 versus $513.7 million in 2022. About Main Street Bank: Main Street Bank was founded in June 2001, and we are proud that our bank is locally owned and managed. We know that a locally based bank staffed by seasoned banking professionals has a greater understanding of what local people need. Our customers know that the best bankers in the Ohio Valley work at Main Street Bank.
