 COGNETA GREEN SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. is glad to announce our strategic partnership with WE LEAD COMTECH INC., one of the most dedicated and outstanding product supply & services company in the Power Generation & Distribution industry in the Philippines & SEA. With the sets of goals in common, Cogneta Green Solution and We Lead Comtech will strive to work towards valuable ideas and provide innovative solutions for customers through upcoming cognitive collaboration offerings.  What to look forwards to in this partnership? Cogneta Green and We Lead Comtech share a common goal of providing the most useful & technology-enabled solutions that will enable customers to maintain their renewable power assets for the complete asset lifecycle. We Lead Comtech is a well-known player in the supply of components such as Power Meters, Flow meters, along with myriad instrumentation sensors to cater to Power, Industrial & water setups. In addition to product supplies, We Lead Comtech is equipped with technical personnel to provide consultancy & commissioning services to Renewable Energy EPCs, & Generation/Distribution companies. Cogneta Green Solutions provide new age technology & IoT 4.0-enabled Software & Hardware solutions to digitalize Renewable plants (Solar/Wind/BESS/Hybrid) with a SCADA comprehensive & ANN-ML-based Analytics solution. Cogneta’s Technical team has vast experience in providing turnkey solutions for implementing networks that in turn help digitalize the client workflows. Cogneta also provides end-to-end engineering & commissioning services to implement such solutions & help customers track asset performance & downtimes.  This collaborative initiative led by Cogneta Green Solutions & We Lead Comtech, finds synergy in creating an end-to-end ecosystem for the clients & be a one-stop solution for their Renewable/Industrial project requirements starting from Electrical/Instrumentation supplies, SCADA, ANN-ML based analytics/prediction Cloud solution, IoT 4.0 Hardware solutions -Datalogger, Zero Export, DGPV solutions, project EPC & Commissioning consultancy & services.   Product & Services offered through this initiative at a glance:  1.     Power Meters Supply for Generation/Distribution EPCs 2.     Water Flow Meters & Instrumentation Sensors Supply  3.     POS, Billing, and Sales automated software solutions for Companies  4.     SCADA Monitoring & Control solutions for the Renewable/Energy Industry (Solar/Wind/BESS/C&I Rooftop)  5.     IoT 4.0 enabled Datalogger, Zero Export & DGPV synchronization control solutions for C&I & rooftop solar hybrid installations.  6.     ANN-Machine learning-based Power forecasting, Downtime Performance tracking software cloud/on-premises solutions for EPCs/O&Ms 7.     Turnkey Commissioning & Engineering services for EPCs & O&M companies for Greenfield and Retrofit projects & much more……   In upcoming partnership projects, we would like to support customers with the most cost-effective and practical technology-enabled solutions. Customers can get attractive offerings from both Cogneta Green Solutions & We Lead Comtech when purchasing our products and services. More than that, we promise to continue delivering consistent AFTER-SALES support for technical issues whenever customers need it.
