 Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT) is eager to hire an experienced professional looking to advance their career into a leadership role by serving as our next Mobility & Technology Manager for the Management of Mobility division. This position is responsible for the coordination and planning of integrated transportation programs and projects in the Central Puget Sound region. In addition, this position serves as the agency’s lead for the Central Puget Sound region to facilitate a broad range of creative, new solutions to improve safety and travel mobility during high-impact construction closures, incidents, weather and special events. The successful candidate will establish and maintain close and collaborative relationships other WSDOT regions and programs, as well as cultivate and maintain relationships with external public and private partners including cities, counties, transit agencies and technology companies.
