Former TV anchor Dominique Sachse is launching her own podcast after retiring in 2021. Sachse, an Emmy award-winning journalist for Houston, Texas’ KPRC, made the announcement on her popular YouTube channel. Merging her broadcast experience and YouTube career, Over 50 & Flourishing with Dominique Sachse will focus on topics relevant to the mid-life stage. The first episode, out Monday, May 1, features an interview with Houston-based life coach Peter Remington. Audio will be available on all major podcast platforms, while a video version will be hosted on her YouTube channel.
