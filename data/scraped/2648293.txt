The Board approved the Inventory and Ticketing Software Renewal in the amount of $35,585.04 for 3 years, and approved 10 new courses for Liberal High School. The courses approved are: Media and Public Relations, Banking and Finance, Work Experience in A&C, World Civ II, Ag Internship, Agriscience, Animal Science, Equine Science, Intro to Agriculture, and Small Animal Care. All ten courses were approved by the Curriculum Committee on Oct. 5, 2023.  The Board also heard the first reading for board policies BDA, CEC, DB, and DC.
