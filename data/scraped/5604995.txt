 Are you looking for a promising investment opportunity in the upcoming IPO market?  If yes, then you might want to check out Tata Technologies Limited, a global engineering, and digital services company that is a subsidiary of Tata Motors. Tata Technologies is planning to launch its IPO soon, The Tata Technologies IPO opening date End of August to Mid of September 2023, and the closing date End of August to Mid of September 2023,. The IPO price range is expected to be around Rs 280 to Rs 285 per share. The company was founded in 1989 as the automotive design unit of Tata Motors and became a separate entity in 1994. Tata Technologies is a leader in providing product development and digital solutions to global original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and their tier-1 suppliers in the automotive, aerospace, and heavy construction machinery industries. Tata Technologies has a strong presence in key markets such as North America, Europe, India, China, and Southeast Asia, with over 11,000 employees across 18 global delivery centers. Tata Technologies has a diversified revenue mix, with 88% of its revenue coming from services and 12% from products and value-added reselling. The company also has a high proportion of repeat business from its clients, indicating customer satisfaction and loyalty. Tata Technologies has shown consistent growth in its financial performance, with a 15% year-on-year increase in revenue and a 9% increase in net profit for the nine months ended December 2022. The company also has a healthy balance sheet, with a net worth of Rs 2,752.83 crore as of December 2022. Tata Technologies is backed by the Tata Group, one of the most respected and trusted conglomerates in India and the world. The company benefits from the Tata brand value, corporate governance, and business ethics.
