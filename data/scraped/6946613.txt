COLUMBIA, S.C. (WIS/Gray News) - A zoo in South Carolina has welcomed a baby koala just in time for the new year. On Wednesday, the Riverbanks Zoo and Garden shared photos of its new addition on social media, writing, “It’s a girl.” Zoo officials said the baby koala also passed her first veterinarian checkup. According to the Riverbanks Zoo, the public will soon be able to help the team choose a name for the baby. Copyright 2022 WIS via Gray Media Group, Inc. All rights reserved.
