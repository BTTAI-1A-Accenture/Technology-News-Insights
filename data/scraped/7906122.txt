BYD to showcase luxury line, while Toyota to unveil new electric models SHANGHAI -- Auto Shanghai, one of the world's biggest automobile shows, opens Tuesday with makers set to unveil a vast slate of new electric vehicle models and technologies in the country that boasts the largest EV market. The Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition, as the show is formally known, features more than 1,000 Chinese and foreign automakers and parts manufacturers across a 360,000-square-meter space.
