Publisher 3D Realms and Pinstripe developer Atmos Games have announced "BioShock meets Willy Wonka" first-person shooter, Twisted Tower, for PC via Steam.  View the announcement trailer below:  View the developer log below:  Read details on the game below: BioShock meets Willy Wonka. Climb the ever-changing 1920s Twisted Tower game show! Every twisting floor has a variety of paths to choose, horrifying enemies to slaughter, and clues about your dark past. Can you get to the top and win the heart of the love of your life? Step right up into the Twisted Tower, the world’s bloodiest game show! Are you lucky enough to ascend the treacherous floors, confront the tower demons, and reach the top? Features: It can’t be… you found a Velvet Ticket!
