 CEBU CITY – With the continuous goal of providing holistic experiential learning for IT students and aspiring developers, DNA Micro opened its doors to Mindanao State University-Iligan Institute of Technology (MSU-IIT) Computer Engineering students yesterday, June 15, for an educational tour.  MSU-IIT’s main objective was to allow students to explore diverse learning experiences and cultures, technological advancements, company operations, software development, and communication media used in the industry. And with DNA Micro continually establishing long-term partnerships with universities both in and out of Cebu City, the educational tour paved the way for stronger collaborations, with DNA Micro professionals offering practical and real-world knowledge to the young learners. During the educational tour, 13 students from the College of Computer Engineering sat down for a program before proceeding with the educational excursion proper. Leo Lorenzo Soon, Software Engineer, discussed the DNA Training Curriculum that covered JavaScript, nodeJS, TypeScript, and ReactJS. To give the students insight into mobile app development, they immediately proceeded to the project immersion for InstaProtek, headed by  Junior Project Manager Roy Joseph Argumido. After the discussions, the students toured each team, allowing them to ask questions about the team's daily grind.  To cap off the two-hour tour, Shem Alexis Chavez (Senior Mobile Developer), Roy Joseph Argumido (Junior Project Manager), Krizza Mae Montalban (Training Coordinator), and Cedric Castro (Junior Software Engineer) offered indispensable career advice to students to help them navigate the professional world that they are set to enter a few months from now.  ABOUT DNA MICRO DNA Micro Software Inc. is a technology services company that offers collaborative solutions to assist clients in developing cost-effective technological solutions from inception to delivery and support. The company helps clients grow by creating innovative software solutions tailored to their unique needs so they can continue thriving in the digital business landscape.  ABOUT MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY – ILIGAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY MSU-IIT is dedicated to academic excellence with a commitment to the holistic development of the individual and society. The academe is known for its excellence in science and technology, passion, and dedication to extensive research and community involvement. Founded in 1968 under the provision of Republic Act (RA) 5363, MSU-IIT is a state university where solid integration exists between different local religions and cultures.
