 GE Australia and New Zealand is proud to announce Resinc Solar as the winner of the Platinum Retailer of the Year Award 2022. The initiative was launched to recognise and celebrate the highest-performing companies for both residential and commercial sales of GE's range of premium solar inverter products. Resinc Solar stood out among the competition due to their outstanding achievements in sales and customer service, as well as their commitment to promoting and delivering sustainable energy solutions. The company's dedication to delivering innovative, high-quality solar products and exceptional customer service has truly set them apart in the industry. As a reward for this accomplishment, Resinc Solar received a custom-made trophy crafted from "Ecostone," a sustainable material that aligns with our eco-friendly values. This recognition also grants them exclusive access to a suite of enhanced resources to grow their business and leverage the globally renowned GE brand. The GE Platinum Retailer program in Australia and New Zealand sets high standards for retailers to meet annually, ensuring elite-level service offerings for GE solar inverter products. In exchange, qualified Platinum Retailers receive access to an exclusive suite of enhanced resources covering sales, support, and marketing to drive growth while leveraging the globally renowned GE brand. This program is designed to empower retailers with the tools and support they need to succeed and deliver the best possible experience to customers. Leigh Storr, CEO of Resinc Solar, commented: “We've been using GE inverters for the past few years. They have been exceptional products that align perfectly with our mission as a social enterprise. Our customers love GE inverters, and we are pleased to say that they have been a valuable addition to our solar solutions. The setup process is fast, and the products are well-designed, making them an excellent choice for our customers who prioritise quality and efficiency. We are thrilled to be recognised as the Platinum Retailer of the Year by GE Australia and New Zealand, and we look forward to continuing our partnership to provide sustainable energy solutions and exceptional service.” Gopinath Mani, Business Development Manager for Australia and New Zealand, commented: “Congratulations to the Resinc Solar team for their impressive achievement and continued dedication to delivering high-quality solar products and exceptional service. The GE Platinum Retailer program is designed to empower retailers with the resources and support they need to succeed, and it's clear that Resinc Solar has taken full advantage of these offerings to drive their growth and success. As we continue to develop the program, we're excited to see more retailers following in their footsteps and delivering exceptional service to customers across Australia and New Zealand.” https://www.resinc.com.au/ END.
