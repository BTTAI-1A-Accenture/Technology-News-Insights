Athlete Tech Group, whose Training Ground app helps pro athletes transition to a new career after sports, has partnered with Unstoppable Domains to provide digital identity ownership and verification in Web3. Unstoppable Domains is also donating $100,000 of credits to Athlete Tech Group participants. The blockchain-based technology from Unstoppable Domains will help athletes build and maintain their online brands and create their own NFTs in a transparent and tamper-proof manner. One of Unstoppable Domains’ top executives is COO Sandy Carter, a former AWS SVP who led the tech giant’s entry into sports.
