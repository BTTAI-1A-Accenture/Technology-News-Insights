The Florida State Legislature will have a special session next week in which lawmakers are expected to see bills on the Reedy Creek Improvement District and other topics, according to a memo from the president of the state Senate.  Florida Senate President Kathleen Passidomo said in a Friday memo to all senators that she and Florida House Speaker Paul Renner have plans to "convene the Legislature in Special Session B, beginning on Monday." The special session comes after coordination between the Florida State Legislature and Gov. Ron DeSantis’s office, according to the memo.
