JONES COUNTY, Miss. (WDAM) - The Jones County Sheriff’s Department is asking for information as an ATV was reported stolen recently. According to JCSD, a gray and orange 2021 Polaris Sportsman 850 High Lifter edition ATV with 20-inch black and chrome rims, 33-inch tires and a Bluetooth speaker mounted on the front. The ATV was reported stolen from the 900 block of Trace Road overnight, according to the sheriff’s department.
