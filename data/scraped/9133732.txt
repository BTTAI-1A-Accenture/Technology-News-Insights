WICHITA, Kan. (KWCH) - Spirit AeroSystems is looking to hire hourly employees for multiple positions. The aviation manufacturer will hold a job fair at its headquarters in Wichita on Saturday, Feb. 11, from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.  Candidates will have the opportunity for interviews and on-the-spot job offers. Benefits include competitive pay, tuition assistance and opportunities for advancement. For certain manufacturing job codes, Spirit is offering paid on-the-job training.
