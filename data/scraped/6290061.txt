Modernizing Health Care Brands through Consumer and Retail Approaches Today’s modern health care environment is changing. Rising health care costs and insurance deductibles are incentivizing consumers to take more control over how they spend their health care dollars. Increasing transparency in health care quality, price, and service is empowering consumers to shop for providers that best suit their needs, and digitization across industries is driving individuals to reassess how they engage with their health care providers and their expectations for that experience.
