ECM Investors LLC, parent company of ECM Industries LLC, a manufacturer and supplier of electrical products for the construction and maintenance, irrigation, and landscape supply and utility markets, has signed an agreement to be acquired by nVent Electric plc, a provider of electrical connection and protection systems. With more than 10,000 employees at more than 130 sites worldwide, nVent designs, manufactures, markets, installs, and services products that connect and protect mission-critical equipment, buildings, and essential processes.
