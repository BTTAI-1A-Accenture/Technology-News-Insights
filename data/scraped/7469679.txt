Latam FX Talking: Carry trade unwind recommends caution Higher volatility led by US rates is causing trouble for the carry trade. Having been big beneficiaries of these flows, high-yield Latam FX is now under pressure. Losses may extend a little further, but the fundamentals of Brazil and Mexico look good, and their currencies should be supported on dips.
