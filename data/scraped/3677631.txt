There are multiple ISS missions in August. Paul Hennessy/SOPA Images/LightRocket via Getty Images The commercial and government space scene is buzzing as new launch programs take off and existing ones grow. August is going to be a busy month with multiple missions scheduled around the world. Richard Branson’s Virgin Galactic (SPCE) is expected to launch its first tourism spaceflight from New Mexico; SpaceX has several satellite deliveries scheduled and a Dragon Crew mission to the International Space Station (ISS) on the calendar. Russia’s government space agency Roscosmos, despite the ongoing war with Ukraine, also has a cargo ISS mission planned.
