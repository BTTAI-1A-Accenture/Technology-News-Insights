 Managed IT services are a type of service offered by an IT consultancy or vendor where the company takes on responsibility for the monitoring, management and/or problem resolution for the customer's IT systems and infrastructure. The managed IT services provider typically provides a range of services, including monitoring and managing servers, networks, applications, databases and storage, backup and disaster recovery, and more. The managed IT services provider is responsible for maintaining the customer's IT environment and ensuring it is secure and running smoothly. This allows the customer to focus on their core business activities, while the managed IT services provider takes care of the IT infrastructure.
