   Date (MENAFN- PR Newswire)  LOS ANGELES, April 25, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- the law offices of frank r. cruz announces that investors with substantial losses have opportunity to lead the securities fraud class action lawsuit against Fidelity National Information Services, Inc. ("Fidelity" or the "Company") (NYSE:  fis ).
  Class Period:  February 9, 2021 – February 10, 2023  Lead Plaintiff Deadline:  May 5, 2023
If you are a shareholder who suffered a loss, click here to participate.
The complaint filed alleges that, throughout the Class Period, Defendants failed to disclose to investorsthat: (1) the integration of Worldpay was not ahead of schedule; (2) the integration of Worldpay was not successfully completed during the Class Period; (3) the increases in revenue synergies were not driven by the Worldpay integration; and (4) as a result, Defendants' positive statements about the Company's business, operations, and prospects were materially misleading and/or lacked a reasonable basis at all relevant times.
