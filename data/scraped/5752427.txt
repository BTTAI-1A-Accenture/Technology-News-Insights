Jackson Tuvici is ready to excite the people of Labasa, with unique events along with entertainment gigs this year. The energetic social worker believes Labasa has been long deprived regularly of fun-filled social events. “Apart from the Duavata Carnival and Friendly North Festival, we do not have much entertainment,” said Tuvuci who dubbed himself home boy.
