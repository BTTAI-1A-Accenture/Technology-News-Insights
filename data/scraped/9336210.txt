Feb 9, 2023 The history and importance of land-grant university programs will be the topic of the next William Holmes McGuffey Historical Society’s Memories of a Lifetime lecture. Speaker Haley Shoemaker serves as the Columbiana / Mahoning agriculture and natural resources educator for the Ohio State University Extension Office in Canfield. During her six-year career, she has worked with area farms, agricultural agencies and communities to bring relevant university research to a practical level that can be implemented on the farm.  The program starts 10 a.m. Feb. 18 at Denny’s Restaurant meeting room, 4020 Belmont Ave., Liberty. Admission is $5 for members and $10 for nonmembers. Reservations are recommended and can be made by calling 330-726-8277.
