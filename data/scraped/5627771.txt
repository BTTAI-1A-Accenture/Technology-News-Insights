   Date (MENAFN- ForPressRelease)  The Titan 100 program recognizes St. Louis' Top 100 CEOs & C-level executives. Representing both the private and public sectors, these Titans of industry demonstrate exceptional leadership, vision, passion, and influence in their field.

“The Titan 100 are changing the way that business is done in St. Louis. These preeminent leaders have built a distinguished reputation that is unrivaled in their field. We proudly recognize the Titan 100 for their efforts to shape the future of the St. Louis business community. Their achievements create a profound impact that makes an extraordinary difference for their customers and clients across the nation.” says Jaime Zawmon, President of Titan CEO.

“Creating meaningful change in communities takes many individuals pioneering many solutions and working together at all levels. I'm humbled to have been chosen for the second time - to improve outcomes and make real progress with the other Titan 100 leaders.“ added Srikant Chellappa.
