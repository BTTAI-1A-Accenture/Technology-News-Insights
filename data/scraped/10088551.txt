Police released this surveillance camera image of a vehicle sought in connection with a hit-and-run crash Wednesday in Bridgeton that left a 10-year-old girl hospitalized in critical condition. UPDATE: Driver charged in hit-and-run that crash that seriously injured 10-year-old girl A 10-year-old Bridgeton girl is hospitalized in critical condition after she was struck by a hit-and-run driver Wednesday night in Cumberland County. The girl was trying to cross East Broad Street near a Wawa in Bridgeton around 7 p.m., according to police. She was struck by a white SUV that fled the scene. The vehicle was last seen heading north on the Bank Street Extension heading toward East Commerce Street. The girl was airlifted to Cooper University Hospital with serious injuries and was listed in critical condition as of Thursday morning, police said. Anyone with information about the incident is asked to contact Bridgeton Police Detective Chris Zanni at 856-451-0033. Anonymous tips may be shared online at bpd.tips.
