Chloe Riley | Mar 06, 2023 The Fresh Market, Inc. has launched a shoppable version of its magazine, previously available in print and online, in a new, digital-only format. The digital mag is yet another step for the Greensboro, N.C.-based company into the world of shoppable content — last month Fresh Market launched a retail media network for vendors seeking to post shoppable ads during the retailer’s livestreamed online broadcasts.
