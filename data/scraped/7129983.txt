 Raytheon-Lockheed Martin Javelin JV, Tucson, Arizona, was awarded a $13,488,810 modification (P00168) to contract CW31P4Q-19-C-0038 for the Javelin Missile System. Work will be performed in Tucson, Arizona, with an estimated completion date of July 29, 2024. Fiscal 2022 missile procurement, Army funds in the amount of $13,488,810 were obligated at the time of the award. Army Contracting Command, Redstone Arsenal, Alabama, is the contracting activity.
