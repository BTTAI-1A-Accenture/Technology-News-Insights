News The global cryptocurrency market cap was at $1.13 trillion on Wednesday morning, up by 0.07 per cent in the past 24 hours. Pepe PEPE was the most trending cryptocurrency, while Decentraland MANA was the top gainer and Kava KAVA was the top loser The global cryptocurrency market cap was at $1.13 trillion on Wednesday morning, up by 0.07 per cent in the past 24 hours, even as the total trading volume decreased by 7.52 per cent to $27.87 billion. Pepe PEPE was the most trending cryptocurrency, down by 3.71 per cent to $0.000001671 from the previous day. Its 24-hour trading volume was $264.05 million. Decentraland MANA was the top gainer, up by 11.09 per cent to $0.5172 over the previous day. Its 24 hour trading volume was $96.64 million. Kava KAVA was the top loser, down by 6.04 per cent to $0.9286. DeFi fell by 7.78 per cent to $2.17 billion, Coinmarketcap.com reported. Cryptocurrency Prices Bitcoin: Bitcoin’s price increased by 0.02 per cent to $27,096.97. Its 24-hour trading volume was $12.16 billion. It is currently ranked number 1 on Coinmarketcap, based on market cap. Bitcoin’s market dominance stood at 46.36, down by 0.05 per cent over the last 24 hours. Ethereum: Ethereum’s price increased by 0.71 per cent to $1,825.27. Its 24-hour trading volume was $5.24 billion. Tether: Tether was down by 0.03 per cent to $1 in the last 24 hours. Its 24-hour trading volume was $19.39 billion. It is ranked third on Coinmarketcap. Other Altcoins Solana’s (SOL) price increased by 0.54 per cent to $20.98 in the last 24 hours. Avalanche was trading at $15.00, up by 0.29 per cent, while its 24-hour trading volume stood at $110.80 million. Cardano (ADA) was up by 2.08 per cent to $0.3727. It was ranked seventh, with a 24-hour trading volume of $180.85 million. Meme Coins Dogecoin (DOGE) fell by 2.15 per cent, with a 24-hour price of $0.07322. Shiba Inu was up by 0.10 per cent to $0.000008814. Decentralised Finance DeFi coin was trading at $0.01689, up by 0.67 per cent over the previous day. Yearn.Finance was up by 0.52 per cent to $6,815.93 in the last 24 hours, while its 24 hour trading volume was at $14.19 million.
