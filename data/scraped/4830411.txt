September 14, 2023 at 11:04 AM  1 minute read Insurance Litigation Health insurer Cigna was sued on Sept. 12 in Delaware Court of Chancery in the wake of media reports that the company uses an automated system to deny claims in bulk without review by medical professionals. The lawsuit, brought by Glancy Prongay & Murray and Cooch & Taylor on behalf of a Cigna shareholder, seeks access to related records for the purpose of investigating mismanagement or breaches of fiduciary duty by Cigna’s directors and officers. The case is 2023-0928-, Charles R. Blackburn v. The Cigna Group. This suit was surfaced by Law.com Radar, a source for high-speed legal news and litigation updates personalized to your practice.
