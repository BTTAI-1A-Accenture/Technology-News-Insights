Cognizant has won a 10-year services agreement valued at approximately $1 billion from CoreLogic, a global property information, analytics solutions provider.
“Cognizant has been an invaluable partner for many years in helping build our platforms — giving clients the data-driven technology solutions they need to navigate business processes,” said Abe Kuruvilla, Chief Information Officer at CoreLogic.
