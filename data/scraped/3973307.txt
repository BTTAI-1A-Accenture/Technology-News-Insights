 The planned area of Yunhan Digital Town in Northern Shenyang New District (Huishan Economic Development Zone) is about 4.9 square kilometers. Aiming at implementing the strategic deployment of "digital Liaoning" and "Digital Shenyang", it aims to build a "digital economic ecological chain" in Shenyang and accelerate the promotion of industrial digitalization and digital industrialization. With big data service industry as the driving core, Yunhan Digital Town has built an industrial direction of "one core and three industries" featuring the coordinated development of digital manufacturing industry, digital enabling industry and digital cultural and creative industry. It has formed a new generation of information technology industrial system mainly featuring electronic information manufacturing industry, software information technology service industry, Internet and related service industry and integrated circuit industry. Integrated circuits, intelligent terminals, mobile Internet, industrial Internet, big data and other industrial chain elements are improving day by day. It is expected that by 2025, the town will have more than 1,000 high-tech enterprises, more than 100 mass entrepreneurship and innovation carriers, and attract more than 10,000 innovation and entrepreneurship personnel. In the future, it will be built into the leading demonstration zone of digital economy in Northeast China and the first city of digital economy in Northeast China, providing rich application scenarios and broad development space for enterprises in digital economy and emerging industries. Yunhan Digital Town has the distinctive characteristics of a modern comprehensive city, and is an important undertaking area for the expansion of the main urban area and industrial transfer. Now the digital economy of the town has begun to take shape, gathering many elements of scientific and technological innovation in Shenyang. In the future, more enterprises will get the opportunity to cooperate with the regional top talents here, and the development potential of the town is worth looking forward to.
