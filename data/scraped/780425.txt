         NEW YORK -- There was a scare in Chelsea when a section of road collapsed Saturday. It happened at the intersection of West 19th Street near Seventh Avenue. First responders received reports of a manhole fire just before 2 p.m. There were no reports of injuries. Con Edison says a primary heater is out of service at the intersection, and crews are making repairs. No customers have been impacted.
