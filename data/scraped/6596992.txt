The food texture market refers to the industry that deals with the production and sale of food ingredients and additives that enhance or modify the texture of various food products. Texture plays a crucial role in the overall sensory experience of food, affecting factors such as mouthfeel, consistency, and perception of quality. Food texture ingredients are used to improve the taste, appearance, stability, and shelf life of food products.
