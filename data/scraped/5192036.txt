Processing.... Christina Mee has been named vice president of mission services at Insight Technology Solutions, where she brings two decades of federal civilian and defense market experience. Mee will oversee business expansion initiatives in strategic planning and communications, program management and cybersecurity, Insight said Tuesday in a LinkedIn post. Most recently, she served as vice president of federal civilian programs at Amyx, a systems integrator and cybersecurity company acquired by Tetra Tech (Nasdaq: TTEK) in January. She previously managed communications for the federal business at Savvee Consulting in VP role. Her career with the Chantilly, Virginia-based IT consultancy spans nearly 11 years. Earlier in her career, Mee worked as a broadcast journalist at the U.S. Army and served in Operation Enduring Freedom in Afghanistan from 2005 to 2006.
