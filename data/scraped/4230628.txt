LIMAY, Bataan: A mother and her two children died while the father and another child were injured when the car they were riding rammed into a tree along the Roman Expressway in Barangay Alangan here at dawn on Saturday, August 19. Limay police chief Maj. Dennis Duran identified the victims as Dyanne Bebit, 31, and her children Shinjie Mae Javier, 11, and Mark Lenz Javier, 2. Police said the three died on the spot.
