Rakuten Viki’s K-Drama Cafe booth at KCON LA 2023 welcomed a very special guest! Much-loved K-pop girl group NMIXX made an appearance at Viki’s booth at KCON last month, sharing fun interactions with some lucky fans who got to take photos and be served boba by the members. The six NMIXX members also took time to answer fan-submitted questions, dishing on drama roles they want to take on, memorable fans, superpowers they wish to have, what they would like to do for vacation, and more.
