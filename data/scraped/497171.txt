BLOOMINGTON – The Monroe County Public Library will host a grand opening at its southwest branch on Friday, June 9 at 890 West Gordon Pike, near Batchelor Middle School. To celebrate the grand opening, the Library will host a public event for Monroe County residents from 2 p.m. until 7 p.m. that will include food, special events and performances, and opportunities to enjoy the new spaces within the Southwest Branch. The new library is 42,000 square feet including a garage.  The branch will include a teaching kitchen, a teen room, multiple conference rooms, and an amphitheater: a place open to the public, with outdoor seating and vegetation. 1340 AM WBIW, Bedford’s Place To Talk.
