Climate Tracker opens a new call for applications for its Climate Journalism Mentorship Program. In an initial phase, participants will engage in an online discussion on energy transition and climate change. They will then submit a pitch for a story to be developed during the mentoring program. The Climate Tracker team will select two proposals from each country. From September to December 2024, the six chosen journalists will benefit from group learning sessions, one-on-one mentoring, and a US$900 grant to produce three stories on energy transition in Latin America, focusing on challenges in Chile, Colombia and Peru. Selected applicants will also have networking opportunities. The deadline is Aug. 28.
