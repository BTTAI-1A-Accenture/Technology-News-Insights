Breaking News
  The Golden Globes are trying to make a comeback in 2023. The 80th Annual Golden Globe Award nominations were announced on December 12 from the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Los Angeles. The best of the best in film and TV over the past year are being recognized. Comedian Jerrod Carmichael is set to host the live Golden Globes ceremony, which will air January 10 on NBC and Peacock. The 2022 Golden Globes were notoriously not televised after NBC dropped the awards show from its lineup due to ongoing criticism of the Hollywood Foreign Press Association.
