 Gurugram, Apr 1 (PTI) Some unidentified fraudsters allegedly duped a company of Rs 35 lakh by apparently tampering with its online payment gateway system, police said on Saturday.


 Ankit Rawat, the national head of operations at Parviom Technologies Pvt. Ltd., in his complaint said that his company uses the payment gateway services of Cashfree, which was breached resulting in 35 UPI transactions after getting unauthorised access to the systems through its dashboard.


 “Through the fraudulent transactions a total of around Rs 35 lakh were transferred in the different bank accounts,” Rawat said in the complaint.
