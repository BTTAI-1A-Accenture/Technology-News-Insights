Global Automotive Data Cables Market: Growth Factors The growing demand for connected and electric vehicles is a significant driving factor for the automotive data cables market. Connected vehicles require extensive data communication between various onboard systems, including infotainment, navigation, telematics, and vehicle diagnostics. Electric vehicles rely on data cables to transmit critical information related to battery management, powertrain efficiency, and charging infrastructure. As the popularity of connected and electric vehicles continues to rise, the need for advanced automotive data cables with higher bandwidth, enhanced durability, and improved data transfer capabilities increases, thereby driving the growth of the market.
