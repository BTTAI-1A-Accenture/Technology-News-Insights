DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The "Submarine-Launched Missile Market by Type (Submarine-Launched Ballistic Missile, Sea-Launched Cruise Missile), Application, and Region 2023-2028" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

  
The global submarine-launched missile market size reached US$ 313.7 Million in 2022. Looking forward, the publisher expects the market to reach US$ 431.0 Million by 2028, exhibiting a growth rate (CAGR) of 5.4% during 2023-2028

 
The global submarine-launched missile market is primarily driven by several key factors. First, increasing geopolitical tensions and security concerns among nations have led to a growing demand for submarine-launched missiles. These missiles offer a strategic advantage by providing covert and survivable platforms for long-range offensive capabilities, enhancing deterrence capabilities, and maintaining a credible defense posture.

 
Second, the modernization of naval forces is a significant driver of the market. Many countries are investing in the modernization of their naval fleets, including the procurement of advanced submarines equipped with submarine-launched missile systems. Upgrading submarine fleets with improved missile technology helps nations maintain or gain a competitive edge in naval warfare and power projection.

 
Third, submarine-launched ballistic missiles (SLBMs) with nuclear warheads play a critical role in a nation's nuclear deterrence strategy. Several countries are investing in submarine-launched missile systems to strengthen their nuclear deterrence capabilities.

 
Fourth, extensive research and development (R&D) activities are being undertaken by key players in the market to enhance the capabilities of submarine-launched missiles. This includes improving factors like range, accuracy, stealth, and payload capacity, which contributes to market growth.

 
Fifth, submarine-launched missiles come in various types, including ballistic missiles and cruise missiles. Ballistic missiles offer high speeds and the ability to carry nuclear or conventional warheads over long distances. Cruise missiles, on the other hand, provide versatility, stealth, and precision strike capabilities.

 
Finally, regional dynamics also play a significant role in the market, with North America being a major player. The region's presence in the submarine-launched missile market is attributed to factors such as national security concerns, modernization efforts in naval forces, and the development of enhanced submarine-launched missiles.

 
In summary, the submarine-launched missile market is influenced by a combination of factors, including geopolitical tensions, naval modernization, nuclear deterrence strategies, R&D activities, and the diverse capabilities offered by different types of submarine-launched missiles.
