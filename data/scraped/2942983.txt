The much-awaited annual Central Economic Work Conference (CEWC) concluded on Friday, outlining the Chinese government's main economic objectives and policy plans for 2023. In the conference readout, the senior leadership put the “stabilization” of growth, the labor market and inflation as its top priorities, and urged government agencies to improve collaboration and increase macro policy support.
