An auto dealership property along Interstate 77 in Jackson Township has been sold for $1.3 million, according to the latest real estate transfers filed with the Stark County Auditor's Office. WMP Holdings LLC of Plain Township purchased the property that once housed Jeff's Motorcars of North Canton at 6151 Dressler Road NW from JD Real Estate Investments Inc. for $1,367,000. Jeff's Motorcars operates at 5732 Dressler Road NW. WMP Holdings is owned by Jeff and Wendy Leeders, according to county records, who also operate Daystar Chrysler Dodge Ram in Malvern and Daystar Ford of Garrettsville. The Daystar Auto Group website lists the property at 6151 Dressler Road NW as the Daystar Autosphere.
