   Date (MENAFN- GlobeNewsWire - Nasdaq)  ELKHART, Ind., Dec. 15, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- THOR Industries, Inc. (NYSE: THO) today announced that its Board of Directors approved, at its December 15, 2022 meeting, the payment of a regular quarterly cash dividend of $0.45 per share. The regular cash dividend is payable on January 13, 2023, to shareholders of record at the close of business on December 30, 2022.   About THOR Industries, Inc.   THOR is the sole owner of operating companies which, combined, represent the world's largest manufacturer of recreational vehicles.
