 Digital Forensics and Investigation (DFnI), primarily use various procedures, tools and technologies which are essential for conducting thorough investigations, analyzing digital evidence, and recovering information from electronic devices and digital systems. These tools aid in preserving the integrity of evidence and providing valuable insights into various types of cyber incidents. Here are some DFnI tools from which I have built a digital forensics lab: Disk Imaging and Analysis: Memory Analysis: Network Analysis: Malware Analysis: File Carving: Registry Analysis: Mobile Device Forensics: Log Analysis: Data Recovery: Virtual Machine Forensics: Steganography Analysis: Database Forensics: The tools advised above are not exhaustive, the selection of tools totally depends on the specific nature of the incident or the purpose of investigation, the type of digital evidence being analyzed, and the investigator's expertise. The important is what how much we stay up-to-date with the latest tools and techniques is crucial due to the evolving nature of digital threats and technologies.
