Portfolio and MAGE have been successfully organising joint events since 2015 – in 2023, our conference is again held as part of the INDUSTRY DAYS’ 10th International Trade Fair and AUTOMOTIVE HUNGARY International Automotive Supplier Trade Fair’s official programme, at the Hungexpo, on the 17th of May.
In the automotive industry, slowly crisis is becoming the new normal... The soaring energy prices and new supply security aspects have turned corporate energy procurement upside down in this energy-intensive sector as well. Everyone is looking for a solution to decrease vulnerability, be it a renegotiation of their contract, dealing with increasingly complicated commerce constructions, or introducing new energy efficiency technologies. Who decides based on what, and how do energy’s consumer and trader sides collaborate? Which are the realistic possibilities for reducing energy dependency and increasing the use of clean energy? What technologies, digitalisation developments, and HR tools can help us find a way out of the crisis, and where will the resources come from?
Burning questions and practical solutions at the Portfolio-MAGE Automotive Industry 2023 conference – listen to the professional programme and also visit the Industry Days and Automotive Hungary trade fairs.
