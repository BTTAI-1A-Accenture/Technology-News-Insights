THE United States Agency for International Development (USAid), launched in Taguig City a web-based monitoring platform for a retail electricity online portal in partnership with the Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) and the Department of Energy (DoE). The US Embassy in Manila said the Competitive Retail Electricity Market (CREM) retail electricity online portal which was launched on April 20, 2023 aims to promote free and fair competition within the energy sector and potentially cut down electricity costs.
