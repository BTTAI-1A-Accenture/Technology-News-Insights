A 58-year-old man from Kitchener, Ont., has died after falling from a cliff on Mount Nemo Conservation Area in Burlington, Halton police say. Media officer Const. Ryan Anderson told CBC Hamilton that emergency services responded to the call around 4 p.m. on Sunday.  Due to a police investigation, Mount Nemo Conservation area will have a heavy police and EMS presence.<br>Please avoid the area^BC Anderson said the man was hiking with friends when he fell. He said the area where the man fell was difficult to access for emergency crews. The man was pronounced dead at the scene, Anderson said.  "It's a tragic situation and we give our condolences to the friends and family," he said.  Audience Relations, CBC P.O.
