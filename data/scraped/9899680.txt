Salman F Rahman, prime minister's private industry and investment adviser, handed over the "ICSB National Award for Corporate Governance Excellence 2021" to Mahin Mazher, managing director of Index Agro Industries, at a function held at Pan Pacific Sonargaon in Dhaka recently, the company said in a statement. Planning Minister MA Mannan, Commerce Secretary Tapan Kanti Ghosh attended the event as special guests and Asad Ullah, president of ICSB, presided over the function.
