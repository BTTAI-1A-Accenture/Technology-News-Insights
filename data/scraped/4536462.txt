TikTok is testing a new feature that allows users to upload 15-minute long videos, in a move to compete with YouTube. Previously, the maximum video length on TikTok was 10 minutes. The new upload limit is being tested in select regions with a limited group of users. This expansion in video length will give creators more time and flexibility.
