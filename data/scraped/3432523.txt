It's delicious and juicy when added garlic and chili sauce. Pork belly can be served with a cold drink. To prepare your yummy pork belly you need the following ingredients. Ingredients Step 1 Combine the first seven ingredients in a medium bowl and stir to mix them. Slice the onions and cut the pork into small pieces. Step 2 Add the pork and the sliced onion into the mixed spices and stir well to coat. In a frying pan add the pork and cook it while stirring occasionally until it's cooked through and crispy at the edge. Step 3  When ready transfer the pork into a clean plate and garnish with sesame seed and green pepper. Then serve it when still hot and enjoy it with a soft drink.
