Wallen lost his voice, according to the big screen at the stadium  Country music star Morgan Wallen canceled Sunday's performance in Oxford because he lost his voice. More than 30,000 fans were estimated to be waiting in Vaught-Hemingway Stadium in Oxford before the announcement was made on the big screen there. Customers were told they would get refunds on their tickets. Wallen performed Saturday to a capacity crowd, with a show lasting about two hours.
