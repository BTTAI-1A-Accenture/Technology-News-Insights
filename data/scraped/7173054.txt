Lumen Christi players warm up before Tuesday's game. JACKSON -- Over and over again in Tuesday’s Interstate 8 boys basketball clash between Lumen Christi and Western, the Titans would build up a lead. Over and over again, the Panthers would come racing back. But Western was never able to get over the top down the stretch, and Lumen Christi held on for a 55-45 win.
