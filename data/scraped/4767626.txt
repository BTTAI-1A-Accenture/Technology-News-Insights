Multiple charities will attend, with the bus offering a place to warm up, a hot drink and space to chat and find out more about upcoming day trip opportunities. The initiative is funded by the Connecting You fund from Devon County Council. The Chatty Bus initiative is a series of day trips which help provide more social contact for people who may be isolated in our communities. With on-board volunteers and friendly conversations, the project takes groups of individuals on day trips across Devon. Connecting You is a new programme of transport initiatives, aiming to help tackle loneliness, and improve wellbeing through transport for the over 55s and young adults.
