May 18, 2023 Friday, May 5, 2023, marked “School Lunch Hero Day,” which celebrates food service staff in school districts across the country. To celebrate, C.C. Ring Elementary School students signed a poster to thank the school’s cafeteria staff. Pictured surrounding Ring kitchen staff Gracie White, Carla Berg, and Megaley Velazquez are Ring students Owen Ramierez, Bryson Peterson, Nathan Smith, Bishop Goodlow, Arabella Washer, Raylean Bailey, Charlee Stuart, and Elliana Langworthy.
