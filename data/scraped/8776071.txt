     The president of Ireland's dog barked at President Biden during his visit to Ireland. President Biden received a somewhat hostile welcome during his visit with President Michael Higgins of Ireland on Thursday — from the Irish president's dog. As the two leaders strolled around Higgins' residence, they approached the president's furry friend, who appeared excited until Biden neared. The Bernese mountain dog, who goes by the name Misneach, immediately began barking and backed away from the group as Biden approached.
