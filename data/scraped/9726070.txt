This technology uses radio waves to identify objects. If you can tap and pay with your credit card, it is RFID-capable. There are wallets and bags on the market that can block RFID scanning. Still, an RFID-protected wallet or bag might help protect you against accidental tap-and-pay transactions while at the register.
