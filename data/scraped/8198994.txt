THE Commission on Population and Development has made a rebrand of its shortened name from "PopCom" to "CPD'' as part of its 55th year celebration in 2024 as the country's lead government agency in implementing the Philippine Population and Development Program. CPD Executive Director and Population and Development Undersecretary Lisa Grace Bersales said Executive Order 71, Series of 2018, signed by then-President Rodrigo Duterte, was the basis for the agency's change of acronym.
