HANOVER, Germany: Manufacturers are hoping for the artificial intelligence revolution to reach their factories, envisaging robots being used to repair complex machinery. There has been an explosion of interest in the uses of AI across many sectors since the launch last year of chatbot ChatGPT from US startup OpenAI.
