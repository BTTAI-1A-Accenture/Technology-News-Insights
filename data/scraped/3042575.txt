Government compiles list of 200 companies with national security potential TOKYO -- Representatives of four Japanese startups involved in emerging technologies with potential defense applications met with government officials here Wednesday to discuss ways to help fledging companies. The startups are involved in such technologies as drones that can fly long distances and robot suits for carrying heavy loads.
