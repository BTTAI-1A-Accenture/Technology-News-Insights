 Bandai produced a new toy replicating the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit from the series One Piece by Eiichiro Oda. Premium Bandai will begin taking pre-orders for the toy on October 2, 2023 in Japan. International shipping will be available. It will cost 4,400 yen ($29.40), tax included. It is scheduled to ship in February 2024. [Thanks, Dengeki!] The Devil Fruit replica is made of PVC and is approximately 180mm x 150mm. The toy will also come with one soda-flavored piece of gum with any purchase. The item will be sculpted and scaled to size, so it is accurate to the fruit’s actual size in the series. The new Bandai Devil Fruit is a replica of the purple Gum-Gum Fruit, which is only one of the various kinds of Devil Fruits found in the series. When eaten, turns a person’s body to rubber. It is eaten by the protagonist Monkey D. Luffy in every adaptation of the story. The new Devil Fruit replica from Bandai will be available to pre-order starting on October 2, 2023 in Japan. The item is expected to ship in February 2024. One Piece is available to stream on Netflix and Crunchyroll. The live-action adaptation is on Netflix. Viz Media handles the manga outside Japan.
