
  A BARROW man is due to appear in court.
 
  42-year-old Adam Kirkham is said to have driven a Vauxhall Movano van on Blake Street while over the limit for Delta-9-tetrahydrocannabinol.
 
  The defendant, of Middle White Close, is also said to have driven the van on November 3 2022 without insurance.
 
  Mr Kirkham is expected before magistrates in Barrow on May 17.
