 “Celebrate” is a unique free cultural experience that brings the history and traditions of people and places from around the world to the Canyon Country Community Center every second Friday from April to September. The next Celebrate event will be held Friday, Aug. 11 from 6 p.m. to 9 p.m. Come “celebrate” Native American Culture featuring California tribes and the Fernandeño Tataviam Band of Mission Indians. The event will feature hands on crafts. All ages welcome. Learnt= to create stone artifacts, basket weaving, identify native plants and their uses and learn how to process acorns.  Grab a bite to eat from the Wildhorse Cafe, enjoy the Mocktail Garden, gather around the outdoor stage to watch performers share their culture through music, dance and storytelling. “Celebrate” aims to ignite curiosity and promote a greater understanding of Santa Clarita’s cultural diversity. You don’t have to travel far to experience the wonders of other places, cultures and traditions. Come Celebrate!
