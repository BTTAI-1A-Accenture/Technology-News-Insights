 Michael Harradence / March 21, 2023  The LinkedIn profile for Deviation Games‘ Derek Sunshine has indicated that the company’s upcoming PlayStation-exclusive project will include both solo and cooperative modes. Back in January last year, Deviation Games announced that it would be entering full production for its new project that year, with a job listing later suggesting it will take the form of a story-driven RPG. In September 2022, the studio announced that its co-founder, Jason Blundell, is leaving the studio.
