Clover Corp. Ltd. is engaged in research and product development of food supplements, pharmaceutical and nutraceuticals. The company focuses on the delivery of bioactive ingredients using proprietary encapsulation technology to produce ready-to-blend products containing tuna oil and other nutritional lipids. Its products include docosahexaenoic acid oils and powders and microencapsulation. The company was founded by Guy Hamish Drummond in 1988 and is headquartered in Sydney.
