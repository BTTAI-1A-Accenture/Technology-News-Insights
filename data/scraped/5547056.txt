  Edmodo, also known as “Facebook for school,” is the premier social media and learning platform for teachers and students alike and is the best tool for any teacher to stay on the cutting edge.  Why choose Edmodo?  With Edmodo, teachers can bring the class online where students are given quizzes, and assignments to her students. Using Edmodo teachers will have everything in place such as managing the class and consolidating all of their activities. Many Edmodians are former teachers and have a sense of how classrooms work, which is crucial when building a platform to use in education. Edmodo does an excellent job of staying connected and collaborating with teachers. It is well-suited for teachers to create closed, monitored groups for students to join with a special code and once all students are in the group, teachers can close it so no other person may join. Once students are within a group, teachers can set expectations and norms and allow for collaborative experiences when students from different classes come together.    Benefits of Edmodo  Edmodo’s unified database is a great feature that lets users measure the progress being made by their students. Educators can see a student’s grades and participation levels with this feature, and they can even see how students react to the activities and discussions being held in the classroom. All this information helps educators find which parts of their lesson students find hard or exciting.  Educators can make sure Edmodo continues to be exciting and engaging, thanks to the system’s capacity to be customized and extended. Applications and widgets can be integrated into their lesson plans, so each experience is new for students. Despite its many benefits, Edmodo also has some potential challenges and drawbacks. One of the main concerns is that students may become too reliant on technology and lose the ability to interact with their peers and teachers in real life. Another challenge is that some students may struggle with digital literacy, which can affect their ability to engage with the platform effectively.  Overall, Edmodo has many benefits and drawbacks, but its potential to enhance teaching and learning is undeniable. With the right approach, teachers can use Edmodo to create engaging, meaningful, and effective learning experiences for their students. Don’t delay, try Edmodo today!
