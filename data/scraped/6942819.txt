To enjoy additional benefits CONNECT WITH US  Published - August 14, 2023 09:37 pm IST - THIRUVANANTHAPURAM A three-day national seminar, ‘Sanskrit and Indian Knowledge Systems’ will be held at the Kerala University Students Centre, PMG, from August 16 to August 18. Gauri Mahulikar, Academic Director, Chinmaya International Foundation, will inaugurate the seminar. Scholars from different parts of the country will present papers at the three-day event. Former Chief Secretary V. P. Joy will be chief guest at the valedictory session.
