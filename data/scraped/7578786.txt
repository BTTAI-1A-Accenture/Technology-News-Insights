Atlantic City’s ISLAND Waterpark, dubbed the “world’s largest” oceanfront water park of its kind, will soon be ready for guests. Bart Blatstein, the waterpark’s developer, held a preview party for family and friends on Thursday. Blatstein cut the “ribbon” — or, in this case, two braids knotted together from the wigs of mermaids — on his latest development.
