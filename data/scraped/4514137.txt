LOWER SOUTHAMPTON TOWNSHIP, PA â€”Two Philadelphia men are wanted by police in six thefts from a township retailer over the past two months, police said. The Lower Southampton Township Police Department reported retail thefts on Sept. 7 and Sept. 4 and Aug. 30, Aug. 15, and Aug. 11 at TJ Maxx on 105 E. Street Rd. in Feasterville. During the investigation, it was determined that the same individual was involved in each incident.  On July 30, and Sept. 7, the same individual committed thefts at the Burlington Store located at 751 Bustleton Pike in Feasterville. Following an investigation into all these incidents, Darryl Foreman, 30, of Philadelphia was identified as being the individual who committed the thefts.   An arrest warrant was issued for Foreman for six counts of retail theft, six counts of receiving stolen property, and three counts of criminal conspiracy.  On Aug. 15, police investigated a theft at the TJ Maxx in which Foreman was accompanied by Sterling K. Warren, 32, also of Philadelphia.   An arrest warrant was issued for Warren for retail theft, receiving stolen property, and criminal conspiracy. Anyone with information on the whereabouts of both suspects should contact the Lower Southampton Township Police Department at 215-357-1234 or submit a tip via Crimewatch.
