South Carolina Highway Patrol says more than a dozen people have been sent to the hospital following a bus crash in Anderson County. The Anderson County Sheriff's Office said the crash happened on Old Williamston Road and Rogers Road just before 7:30 p.m. on Sunday. The sheriff's office confirmed that 14 people were taken to several hospitals, and medical helicopters were used to transport some people.  The Anderson County Coroner's Office has confirmed they were not called to the scene. Stay with WYFF News 4 as we learn more.
