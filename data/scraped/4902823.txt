Firstly, Cloudera provides data Mobility / Portability or whatever you want to call it. This not only optimises resource usage but also reduces energy consumption, thus minimizing the environmental impact of data centers. Secondly, portable cloud-native analytics enable companies to efficiently analyse their data at scale. This can help them make better decisions while still minimizing their carbon footprints. Thirdly, By offering a standard approach to store and access data, which simplifies complex logic and reduces the number of data hops required.
