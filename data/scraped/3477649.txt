International calls can be expensive, especially if you are still using traditional phone services. However, with Google Voice Calling, making low-cost international calls has never been easier. Google Voice allows you to make international calls directly from the Google Voice website or the Google Voice app. This service provides free international calls to some countries and charges low rates for others, depending on your device and location.

How to use Google Voice Calling for International Calls First, you need to sign up for Google Voice. It is free and easy to do so. Once you have an account, you can start making calls. If you are making calls from your desktop on the Google Voice website, simply click the “Make a call” button and enter the number you want to call. If you are using the Google Voice app on your mobile device, follow these simple steps: 1. Open the app and tap on the “Calls” tab 2. Enter the number you want to call using the keypad 3. Tap the “Call” button to initiate the call If you have a Google Voice number, you can also receive calls from international numbers for free. This is a convenient feature for people who frequently communicate with people outside of their country.

Google Voice rates for international calls

Google Voice offers a list of international call rates that vary depending on the country you are calling. For example, calling a mobile number in Mexico costs $0.02 per minute, while calling a landline in the UK costs $0.01 per minute. The rates are competitive, and significantly cheaper than traditional phone rates.

Free calls to some countries

Google Voice also offers free calls to some countries. You can make free calls to the US and Canada from anywhere in the world. Additionally, you can make free calls to countries such as India, China, and Mexico. However, these are only available for calls to landline numbers, and not mobile numbers.

Conclusion: Using Google Voice Calling for international calls is an easy and cost-effective way to stay connected with people from around the world. With its low rates and free calls to some countries, it is an excellent alternative to traditional phone services. Whether you are making personal or business calls, Google Voice is a great option for anyone who needs to make international calls regularly.
