Russellville Lions Club fundraises for fireworks 
May 22, 2023 at 4:00 a.m.
 by
Garrett Fuller
  RUSSELLVILLE, Mo. -- Several all-terrain vehicle (ATV) riders threw dirt high into the air as they demonstrated their skills and helped raise funds for an upcoming fireworks display.
