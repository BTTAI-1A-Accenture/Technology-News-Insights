   Date (MENAFN- PRLog)  -- PulteGroup, one of the nation's leading home builders, proudly announces the opening of new decorated model homes at Double Branch. Located in Clay County, Double Branch is a new construction community offering single-family homes from the mid-$300s."Our new model homes at Double Branch showcase innovative home designs in a prime location," said Justin Cook, Division President of PulteGroup's Northeast Florida Division. "Many of today's buyers are looking for homes in locations that are near outdoor recreation areas and also offer the conveniences of highways and shopping. Our newest single-family home community in Clay County is ideal for families of all ages with its thoughtfully designed homes built with the homeowner in mind."Pulte is building on 50' and 60' homesites at Double Branch with water and preserve views available. Buyers may choose from 12 consumer-inspired home designs, ranging in size from 1,590 square feet to more than 4,000 square feet. Pulte's wide range of home plans include open consumer-inspired floorplans, designer upgrades and spacious modern living. Pulte Homes has Quick Move-In Homes available at Double Branch, which has no CDD fees and low HOA dues. The community includes a Fiber Network that provides fast, reliable, multi-user Internet needed in homes today and in the future.Double Branch residents enjoy a variety of amenities including community parks, a children's playground, a dog park, nature trails and more. The community is a short drive from state parks and recreational areas. Double Branch is zoned for excellent schools in the Clay County Public School District that are less than five miles away. The community is less than one mile from the First Coast Expressway and near shopping and retail centers.Double Branch is located at 3495 Kindlewood Drive in Middleburg.
