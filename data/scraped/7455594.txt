The Government Tool Room and Training Centre (GTTC), Mysuru, has invited applications for admission to M.Tech in Tool Engineering (two years) for the academic year 2022-23 from candidates who have passed B.E in Mechanical/Industrial Production/Automobile/Auto-mation & Robotics Engi-neering and Marine Engineering or those who have written PGCET. The training offered by GTTC is well-received by the industries and the trained personnel from GTTC are employed in the engineering sector within the country and many of them have become entrepreneurs. GTTC, Mysuru Centre is well- equipped with CNC machines, 3D printing and Robots of robotics. GTTC will organise a campus interview for those students who complete the training. For details, visit GTTC, Plot No. 93 & 94, Belagola Industrial Area, Mysuru-16 or call Mob: 92439-89954 or 87621-40256.
