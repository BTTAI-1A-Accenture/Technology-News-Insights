Jul 6, 2023 MORGAN–John L. Trebesch, 93, of Morgan passed away Monday, July 3, 2023 at the New Ulm Medical Center. Funeral services will be held at 10:30 a.m. on Saturday, July 8, 2023 from Zion Evangelical Lutheran Church in Morgan, with burial in Zion Eden Evangelical Lutheran Cemetery, rural Morgan. Visitation will be held Friday, July 7, 2023, from 5 until 8 p.m., at the Zion Evangelical Lutheran Church in Morgan. Visitation will continue on Saturday, from 9:00 until 10:30 a.m., at the church.  Arrangements are with Nelson-Hillestad Funeral and Cremation Service of Morgan.
