Publisher DANGEN Entertainment and developer Pomshine Games have announced virtual reality cooking game Umami Grove for PlayStation VR 2, PC (Steam), and Quest 2. A release date was not announced. A PC demo is available now.  Here is an overview of the game, via DANGEN Entertainment: About Umami Grove is an explorative virtual reality cooking adventure with dynamic controls set in a lush, open world with engaging characters that come to life. Jump, climb, duck, and swing your way through a unique cooking adventure using a powerful movement and interaction system. Forage for ingredients in a visually rich, atmospheric world themed after seasons while completing tasks for quirky NPCs. In Umami Grove, you’ll face peril and hone your cooking for the greatest prize of all: Gold! – in the shape of acorns. And possibly enchanted? Your Culinary Journey Begins!
