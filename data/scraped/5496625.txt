The latest breaking updates, delivered straight to your email inbox. Hamilton County Engineers announced a road closure in Springfield Township for Duke Energy maintenance this week.  The closure will allow crews from Duke Energy to remove utility poles and overhead electrical lines.  According to officials, Summit Road will close between the Springfield Township corporate line and Edgemont Road from Wednesday, April 19 to Thursday, April 20.  The closure will take place each day from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.  During the closure, a detour will be available via Summit Road to Section Road to Edgemont Road and back to Summit Road.
