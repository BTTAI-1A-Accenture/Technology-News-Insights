Tile Hill is a residential suburb in the west of Coventry. It consists of Tile Hill North, Tile Hill South and Tile Hill Village and contains several woods, parks and nature reserves including Tilehill Wood. It was formerly home to a Massey Ferguson/AGCO factory. Tile Hill College later became City College and has been demolished and relocated to a new site in the Swanswell area of Coventry. Tile Hill is partly in Westwood council ward and partly in Woodlands ward.
