Leuze is presenting the smallest contrast sensor it has ever made for the packaging industry.   The new KRT 3C contrast sensor from Leuze (New Hudson, MI) can be flexibly integrated into packaging processes thanks to its multicolor capability and small size. With the KRT 3C contrast sensor, Leuze is presenting the smallest contrast sensor it has ever made for the packaging industry.
