DAVENPORT, Iowa (KWQC) - One man is dead and a teen was injured in a single-vehicle crash in Davenport Monday morning, police say. Davenport police, fire, and Medic EMS responded about 2:50 a.m. Monday to the 5900 block of Brady Street for a crash, according to a media release. Police said a single vehicle was northbound on Brady Street when the vehicle left the road, grazed a pole, and stopped north of 59th Street. The 25-year-old man driving the vehicle was unrestrained and ejected from the vehicle, police said. He was taken to an area hospital by Medic EMS where he died from his injuries. A 16-year-old passenger was also taken to an area hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, according to police. Two other adult passengers were treated at the scene and released.
