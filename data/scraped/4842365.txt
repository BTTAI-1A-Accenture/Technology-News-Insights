   Date (MENAFN- GlobeNewsWire - Nasdaq)  SAN FRANCISCO, May 11, 2023 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) --   okx   , the second-largest crypto exchange by trading volume and a leading Web3 technology company, has issued updates for the day of May 11, 2023.   OKX Ventures Announces investment in Rage Trade   okx ventures , the investment arm of leading crypto exchange and Web3 Technology company OKX, today announced its investment in rage trade , a double-legged trading protocol on the Ethereum and Arbitrum mainnet that offers users the ability to trade ETH perpetual swaps with up to 10x leverage. The protocol is focused on building the most liquid and only omni-chain ETH perp (powered by UNI v3). Its core products and features include its ETH perpetual swap, Omnichain recycled liquidity and yield-generating 80-20 vaults.   OKX Ventures Founder Dora Yue said:  "We are pleased to participate in this investment. Rage Trade offers a leading delta-neutral strategy that can effectively help GLP users manage price volatility risk. In addition, the upcoming v2 version of Rage Trade's perpetual contract is expected to become a dark horse in the decentralized derivatives market with its innovation." For more information, please visit the support center .
