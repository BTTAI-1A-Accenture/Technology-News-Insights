
Canadian Beer News
  CALGARY, AB – Next-door neighbours Annex Ale Project and The Establishment Brewing Company have announced details of a co-hosted “celebration of the undersung cool cats of the beer world – lagers.” Lagerpalooza will be taking place on Saturday April 1st, and will feature “two breweries, fifteen lagers on tap, five new can releases, five collaborations, DJs, carb station and burgers.” The event will be split across two sessions – 12:00 noon to 4:00 PM, and 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM – and the $15 admission includes access to both brewery taprooms and an 11 oz Lagerpalooza branded tasting glass. Beer tokens and food will be available at both locations, although tokens can only be used at the brewery where they’re purchased. Tickets for Lagerpalooza are available online. For more details, see the event page on the Annex Ales website. Source & Photo: Annex Ale Project
