Bloomage Biotechnology Corp. Ltd. is an investment holding company, which engages in the production and sale of raw materials and end products for a diversified range of hyaluronic acid (HA); provides medical beauty products and services in China; and produces HA raw materials. It operates through the Domestic and Overseas Customers segments. The company was founded on April 3, 2006 and is headquartered in Jinan, China.
