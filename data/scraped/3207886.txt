The Lebron James UNINTERRUPTED x Nike LeBron XX collaboration has an official release date. The Lebron XX UNINTERRUPTED collaboration comes in natural tones and the UNINTERRUPTED brand color “Lapis Lazuli Blue” with a black linen-like upper. A translucent top swoosh allows the watercolor-filled bottom swoosh to remain visible and legible while the message “Speak Truth” is easily read on the sneaker’s soles in all caps. Additional design features include a subtle embroidery logo with LeBron James on the inside of the shoe, a subtle black Nike x UNINTERRUPTED x LeBron logo lockup — representing the partnership embroidered over the medial swoosh — and a lime wash finish covers the midsole.
