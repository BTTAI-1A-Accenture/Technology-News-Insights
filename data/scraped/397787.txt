C3 Industries is a private, vertically integrated, multi-state cannabis company that operates in four states in the U.S. Unlike many other Multi-State Operators (MSOs) in the cannabis industry, C3 Industries has primarily built its operations organically and started in competitive, open markets like Michigan and Oregon. C3 Industries has made an impact in the cannabis space through its vertically integrated operations and focus on producing and selling high-quality cannabis products. The company is known for its state-of-the-art facilities, producing the highest quality indoor cannabis flower and extracted products.
