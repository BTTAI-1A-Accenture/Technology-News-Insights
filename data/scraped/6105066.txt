The NFL Preseason has kicked off which means it’s time for New York Giants fans to prepare for the regular season and shop for tickets to home and away games for the 2023-2024 season. The New York Giants will kick off their 2023-2024 season against the Dallas Cowboys on Sunday, September 10 in an NFC East matchup. Fans looking to shop for tickets to this game or any of the games for the upcoming season can do so by using StubHub and VividSeats. The Giants finished out the 2022-2023 season with a 9-7-1 record and third in the division, which has been their best record in the past few years and was significantly better than the previous year (2021) where they finished with a 4-13 record. New York will face the Detroit Lions in a preseason matchup on Friday, August 11. The Giants will host the New England Patriots in Week 12 on Sunday, November 26.
