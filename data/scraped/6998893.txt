Share LAND O'LAKES, Fla. - A Lake Wales man died Wednesday night after crashing a motor scooter into a pickup truck at a Land O’Lakes intersection, according to the Florida Highway Patrol. Troopers say the 43-year-old man was traveling southbound on Livingston Road, north of SR-54, when he went through a red light and collided with a pickup truck that was traveling eastbound on SR-54. The Lake Wales man was taken to an area hospital and died from injuries he sustained in the crash. The driver of the pickup truck, a 62-year-old man from Brandon, was not injured in the collision.
