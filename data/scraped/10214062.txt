Each Friday morning, Meadowlark Quilt Club members are working hard to give back. From 9 a.m. to noon at Sunset Knoll Recreation Center, 820 S. Finley Road in Lombard, these crafty women are doing what they love to do. The Meadowlarks make quilts and other necessities for hospitals, neonatal intensive care units, Ronald McDonald Homes, health department and homeless shelters. This group is always looking to welcome new faces. All seniors and active adults are invited to join in on the fun each week.For more information or want to join, call (630) 620-7322.
