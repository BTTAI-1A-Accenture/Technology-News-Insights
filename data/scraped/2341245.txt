HONG KONG, June 24, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- Winchester Holding Group WCHS today announced the company "Shell Company" status has been removed by OTC Markets, Inc. Simon Wu (CEO of the Company), states "We no longer met the definition of a Shell Company with OTC Markets."
