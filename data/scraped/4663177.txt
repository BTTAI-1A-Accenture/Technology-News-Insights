					Baltimore News				 NORFOLK, Va., March 2, 2023 – Professional esports commentator – or shout caster – Dara M. Gar will be on-hand during the MEAC Esports Championship at the Norfolk Scope Arena on Wednesday and Thursday, March 8-9, in conjunction with the 2023 MEAC Basketball Tournament. Gar will SHOUTcast the conference’s Smash Bros. semifinals and finals, beginning at 1 p.m. on Wednesday, March 8 on the MEAC Esports Twitch page. With the tag Daramgar, Gar is a professional esports commentator who was nominated for the thegameHERS Shoutcaster of the Year award. Gar has a love for fighting game theory and engine quirks like none other. The MEAC Esports Championship will be in-person for the first time on March 8-9, the first two days of the MEAC Basketball Tournament at the Norfolk Scope Arena. The Smash Bros. tournament will run from 1-8 p.m. on March 8, with a pool of $1,400 in prize money on the line.   2530 N. Charles Street Suite 201Baltimore, MD 21218410-366-3900
