 Delhi : The  OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite will go on sale for the first time in India today. The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 and the midrange smartphone were both unveiled at the same time. Both the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite and the smartphone may be purchased via the company's main website and Amazon.com. The smartphone includes a 108-megapixel primary camera, an IPS LCD display with 120 Hz, and a Snapdragon 695 CPU. There are two models of the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite. The first one costs Rs. 19,999 and includes 128GB of UFS 2.2 storage and 8GB of LPDDR4x RAM. The second model, which is the most expensive one, costs Rs. 21,999 and features 8GB of LPDDR4x RAM and 256GB of UFS 2.2 storage. Pastel Lime and Chromatic are the two colour options for the phone. The pastel lime shade is quite lovely and revitalising. The phone can accommodate an additional 8GB of virtual RAM. In addition, the OnePlus Nord Buds 2 have been made available for Rs 2999. There are two colour options for the earbuds: Thunder Grey and Lightning White. The 6.72-inch FHD+ 120Hz refresh rate IPS LCD display of the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G is a sizable feature of this smartphone. The phone's octa-core Snapdragon 695 CPU, 8GB of RAM, and either 128GB or 256GB of internal memory all work together to run the gadget. It comes pre-installed with the most recent OxygenOS 13 skin based on Android 13. The OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G has a 108MP primary camera, a 2MP depth camera, a 2MP macro camera, as well as a 16MP front-facing camera for selfies and video calls, making it ideal for photographers. The smartphone sports a 5,000mAh battery and a USB Type-C charging connection that enables 67W SuperVOOC rapid charging. The smartphone is 8.3mm thick and weighs 195g. In conclusion, the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G is a potent and feature-rich smartphone that is guaranteed to satisfy those seeking a high-end handset with cutting-edge capabilities. This smartphone is a terrific option for individuals who seek the newest and finest technology in a sleek and elegant form thanks to its huge display, potent processor, enough RAM and storage, sophisticated camera system, and rapid charging capabilities.
