Alex Fessel sank four 3-pointers and went three for three from the line in his 21 points as Vernon won on the road, 62-47, over Dover. Ernest Mack put in 15 points while Ben Jurewicz and Robert Nathan added 11 points apiece for Vernon (9-2), which led 35-21 at the half.
