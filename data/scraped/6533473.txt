              Meet Pathman Senathirajah, a trailblazer in the network marketing industry, who shares eight essential building blocks for entrepreneurial success, defying all odds and achieving greatness in the fast-growing world of direct selling.
             The entrepreneurial culture continues to flourish, and the network marketing industry is growing by leaps and bounds. As the youth is growing and evolving every year, the business of direct selling/network marketing is on the rise. As per the stats, the global network marketing industry is projected to reach a staggering 299.76 billion USD by 2025. While network marketing relies on genuine relationships and direct selling, achieving success in the industry can be challenging. Defying all odds, entrepreneur Pathman Senathirajah is one of the prominent names in the network marketing industry. Bringing an experience of over 24 years in direct selling and business leadership, he has been at the top of the game for more than two decades. Over the years, Pathman has seen the highs and lows and has explored different horizons of entrepreneurship. In his remarkable career, Pathman Senathirajah draws insights and shares eight essential building blocks for entrepreneurial success.
