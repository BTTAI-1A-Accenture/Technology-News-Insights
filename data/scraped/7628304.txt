CLEVELAND, Ohio (WOIO) - Clouds continue to hang around Northeast Ohio tonight as temperatures hold steady into the upper 30s. A chance for rain returns overnight. Activity will be scattered in nature. Any light rain clears by sunrise Sunday, but the clouds will stick around the area throughout the day. Overall, it will be a drier start to the work week. Tuesday will bring about more sunshine to the region before more clouds return through the end of next week.
