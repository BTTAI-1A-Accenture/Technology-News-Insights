Mahe, Seychelles – We are thrilled to announce that $TET, the top-performing token on Uniswap, has been listed on the esteemed MEXC Exchange. This listing represents a significant milestone for the Tectum project and opens up exciting opportunities for investors and traders worldwide. The highly anticipated listing on MEXC Exchange took place on July 18th at 3 pm UTC. MEXC Exchange is globally recognized for its robust trading infrastructure, high liquidity, and extensive range of trading pairs. By joining forces with MEXC Exchange, $TET aims to provide its community with enhanced trading capabilities, increased market exposure, and a seamless trading experience. TET has gained tremendous attention in the decentralized exchange (DEX) space due to its exceptional performance on Uniswap, innovative features of SoftNote, and strong community support. The token’s consistent growth and positive market sentiment make it an attractive asset for investors seeking a token with long-term global utility. Through its listing on the MEXC Exchange, TET gains access to a vast network of traders, institutional investors, and crypto enthusiasts. This increased visibility and accessibility will contribute to the further expansion of the $TET ecosystem and its adoption within the broader crypto community. The $TET team has been working diligently to ensure a smooth listing process on MEXC Exchange. They have dedicated themselves to providing ongoing support and maintaining transparent communication channels with the community throughout this exciting phase. By prioritizing the needs of their users, the $TET team aims to foster a thriving trading environment and strengthen the project’s foundations. In conclusion, the listing of $TET on the MEXC Exchange marks a significant step forward for the Tectum project, amplifying its reach and accessibility. This strategic move brings exciting opportunities for investors and traders worldwide and further solidifies $TET’s position as a leading token in the DEX space. The $TET team remains committed to the project’s success, and they invite everyone to join them on this transformative journey toward revolutionizing the world of crypto payments. For more information about TET and the upcoming listing on MEXC Exchange, please visit the official website at www.softnote.com. Stay tuned for further updates on social media channels, including Twitter, Telegram, and Discord, where the $TET community actively engages with its members, sharing news, insights, and fostering a sense of belonging. Tectum, originating from the cybersecurity company CrispMind, is proud to introduce SoftNote, the ultimate Bitcoin/Crypto Layer 2 solution that overcomes the pitfalls of the lightning network. Built on the Layer 1 Blockchain, Tectum sets the standard as the fastest blockchain in the world, boasting a transaction speed of 1.3 million transactions per second (TPS). With SoftNote, users can enjoy instant, trustless, anonymous, and decentralized crypto payments without incurring any fees.
