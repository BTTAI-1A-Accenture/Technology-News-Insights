HANJIN SHIPPING HOLDINGS CO., LTD. is a Korea-based holding company engaged in the management of subsidiaries. The Company started holding business through the spin-off of its marine transportation business on December 1, 2009. As of December 31, 2010, the Company has two subsidiaries: HANJIN SHIPPING CO., LTD., engaged in marine transportation business, and CyberLogitec Co.,Ltd., engaged in information technology (IT) business.
