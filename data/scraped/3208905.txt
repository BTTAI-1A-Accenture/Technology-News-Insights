Deputy President Rigathi Gachagua has made what could be termed his briefest speech since the Kenya Kwanza government came into power. During the Social Protection Conference, in Nairobi, the second in command directly invited President William Ruto to make his remarks, urging that he had nothing much to say. After being invited to the podium, Gachagua alluded that he would not make a speech because the country was more peaceful, and businesses were being run smoothly.
