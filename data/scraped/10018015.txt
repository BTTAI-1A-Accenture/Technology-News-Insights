Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Jeyhun Bayramov and his Turkish
counterpart Mevlut Cavushoglu have held a telephone conversation,
Azernews reports. During the conversation, the ministers discussed issues of
bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Azerbaijan and
Türkiye, as well as the current situation in the region. The parties also exchanged views on other areas of mutual
