BoxLunch is launching a rad lineup of exclusive horror merchandise just in time for the spooky season. The centerpiece of the collection? Horror baseball and hockey jerseys.  Specifically, the collection will include fan-favorite villains from Beetlejuice, Child’s Play, IT, Friday The 13th, Halloween, and more!  “BoxLunch has always been committed to offering new and unique merchandise that speaks to all types of fandoms, and horror is something we hadn’t leaned into previously,” said Rick Vargas, Senior Vice President of Merchandising and Marketing at BoxLunch. “With this new range of horror merchandise, we’re excited to bring a touch of horrifying nostalgia and excitement to our customers, not just during the Halloween season, but all year round.” The all-new exclusive horror merchandise from BoxLunch is now available in-store and online.
