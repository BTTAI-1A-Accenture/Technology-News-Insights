This electronics retailer sells a large array of computer components and accessories. In 1985, brothers John, David, and Randy Fry and a fourth partner, Kathy Kolder, founded Fry's Electronics in Sunnyvale, California to provide a one-stop shopping experience for the hi-tech person's needs. Fry's operates online and in nine states with 34 stores, each decked out in fantasy themed motifs. Despite infamous customer service, shoppers still flock to the chain, which now sells, software, snacks and other items along with the electronics that made it famous.
