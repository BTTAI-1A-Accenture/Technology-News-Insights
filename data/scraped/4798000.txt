GERING, Neb. (KNEP) - The Gering boys and girls soccer teams continue their tough opening part of the 2023 schedule with a contest against Cheyenne Central on Saturday afternoon. Gering boys (0-2) at home and despite a spirited effort came up short to Cheyenne Central 10-0. Meanwhile the Gering girls (0-2) were out in Cheyenne taking on Central. In the end Cheyenne Central holding off Gering 9-0. Gering girls scheduled to return to action Thursday at home against Sterling. Copyright 2023 KNEP. All rights reserved.
